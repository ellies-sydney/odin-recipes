Mushroom Soup


Description

Ingredients

  • mushrooms
  • chicken stock
  • cream
  • salt
  • pepper
  • chopped garlic
  • butter

Steps

  1. Chop mushrooms and cook together with chopped garlic and butter.
  2. Pour some chicken stock and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Once cooked, stop the heat and add some cream.
  4. Add the soup in a blender and blend it.
  5. Add salt and pepper.
