Japanese Curry


Description

Ingredients

  • curry cubes
  • beef mince
  • onion
  • carrots
  • potatoes
  • water
  • sushi rice
  • vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Cook the rice in a rice cooker. 180ml rice + 200ml water.
  2. Cut the veggies into small pieces.
  3. Stir fry the beef mince and veggies using vegetable oil.
  4. Add the water and cook veggies and meat in medium heat. Add curry cubes.
  5. Cook the curry sauce for 15 minutes.
  6. Serve the curry sauce with cooked rice.
