Lasagna


Description

Ingredients

  • tomato sauce
  • beef mince
  • onion
  • milk
  • butter
  • flour
  • salt
  • pepper
  • lasagna sheets
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Heat up the oven at 180 degrees.
  2. Chop the onion and cook together with beef mince in medium heat.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. The meat sauce is ready!
  4. Melt the butter in a saucepan, add sifted flour and then pour some milk. This is a bechamel sauce.
  5. Put the lasagna sheets in the tray, add the meat sauce and the bechamel sauce. Spread some cheese to make is extra cheesy!
  6. Make about 3 layers.
  7. Bake it in the oven for 20 minutes!
