Pesto Pasta


Description

Ingredients

  • basil leaves
  • pine nuts
  • garlic
  • olive oil
  • parmesan cheese
  • salt
  • pepper
  • pasta

Steps

  1. Make the pesto sauce first! Add basil leaves, pine nuts, garlic, olive oil, parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper in a blender and mix.
  2. Cook pasta.
  3. Mix the noodle and the pesto sauce.
  4. For toppings, you can add cooked bacon, prawns, or grilled mushrooms.
